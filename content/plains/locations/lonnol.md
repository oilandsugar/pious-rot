**Population:** 930  
**Landscape:** large central cavern of opal and turquoise stone, quartz covered tunnels off the main cavern, archways of stone built to denote separations of spaces  
**Local legends:** Stone Memory, Spirit Scars  
**Lost Lore:** Gifts of Renewal, The Wandering Rain  
**Leaders:** the Farmer Family, led by Hildan Seeder of Scars, with a vote every 5 years between the members of the family over 50 years    
**Amenities:** seclusion chambers, vault of relics, chiton atelier, spider and leech apothecary   

### Rumours

The Giants keep their history and the history of the animals and plants of the plains by way of speaking with them and converting the tales to craft; painted, carved and woven tablets preserve the history of the plains. Recently, however, the Druids of the farmer family have heard the stones of their cavern homes speaking to them. Their memory is ancient and their concept of time is not apparent but the stones speak of histories giants have yet known. They now request any Menhir or Standing stones from the Ashen lands to attempt to hear their memories and record the lost giants that used to keep them.  

The Roots of the Earth keep the giants safer than the Field of Ash but they are not completely safe themselves. So far Xygrids and husks are the most terrible things met in the depths, but usually they are confined to the caverns below the field. But, in the dark depths of Lonnol, deeper than any cavern, something dark stirs in a now sealed tunnel off the cavern home. The original seeker who found the tunnel never returned, those who searched for her heard a terrible chattering and saw the wall writhe before quickly retreating from the tunnel. If not for the gems of the cave Lonnol would have been entirely abandoned, but for now the Farmer family live near a terror they hope is unable to find them.
