The cavern home of the Gardener family, a wonder in agriculture.Adapting to the reality of the depths of the earth in their own home the Gardeners utilize wood and bone pits to grow large mushroom gardens below the surface, which are carefully curated to avoid toxic and rank mold from developing. The white web of mycelium covers much of the tunnels there giving the walls an almost cloth like look in the low light of the bioluminescent mushrooms.

</div>

**Population:** 3290  
**Landscape:** omaze-like tunnels, multi-tiered halls, limestone and stonework walls, small flute chimneys that let fresh air and moisture into the deep tunnel, mycelium covered corridors  
**Local legends:** Humming Rot, Jinbadda, Wrong Turn Tunnel  
**Lost Lore:** Panacea Flower  
**Leaders:** the Gardener Family, led by Fyrkaf Who Fertilizes the Earth with a vote to vote on a leader after every harvest, after which if a leader is to be voted in the debates and deliberation happens over the span of a year    
**Amenities:** mushroom farms, sunwisps and beetle nursery, fungal druid circle, cave organ
