**Population:** 6540  
**Landscape:** wide mouthed descending cavern covered in curling stalactites, entrance cavern ledges onto main cavern for a balcony like effect, small streams during rainfall, stone walled fortress facing out on the southern plains badlands  
**Local legends:** The Lost Lamb, Trapdoor Den, Badland Trolls  
**Lost Lore:** Wolf Wardens, The Fire Ewe  
**Leaders:** The Shepherd Family, led by Gnellik Tender of the Flock, with a vote every 3 years in times of prosperity, and 6 years in times of hardship    
**Amenities:** armoury, forge, seed shepherd huts, weaving halls, animal pens   

### Rumours

Shepherds want to roam and this instinct is not lost on their children. To try their best to keep their children near the home the Shepherds tell many stories of wolves in the night, spiders in the tunnels and trolls in the badlands. The problem with this is the true mix with the cautionary, and the all too real Trolls are thought of as imaginary. There are 5 of them, large, round faced, flat board and blue. They have no Ill intention but they're curiosity gets the best of them and they have been known to abduct giants to ask them endless questions and are quick to anger if their curiosity is not entertained.   

The Harvest Scythe is hellbent on bringing the giants who raided the outskirts of their encampment, giants who proudly wore the symbol of the Shepherds. Currently the Harvest Scythe is too weak to attack but there is talk on the roads of a southern mercenary company marching their way to the plains. Gnellik won't move any guards from the fortress so is looking for sympathetic outsiders to scout and sabotage this meeting.
