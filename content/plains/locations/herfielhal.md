<div class="flavor">
    
The North Eastern entrance to the Roots of the Earth, though one would miss it if they didn't know where to go between massive roots of ancient trees, a protection granted by an ages old friendship. Herfielhal almost rejects its place in the stone by its wood columned halls and green paved floors that the Arborists need for any sense of comfort in their new home. While the way in is too hidden for trade it allows the Arborists access to the forest they are seeding in the north west and the field there as well as branches to the Gnarltick tunnels in the west.

</div>

**Population:** 6007  
**Landscape:** oblong caverns, pocking of ponds throughout that feed deep roots, in spring snowmelt and rain bring a waterfall down and expand the main basin, lush moss and grasses, wood walls and rooms, serpent stone floors for inner halls  
**Local legends:** Bark Liege, Xygrid chute  
**Lost Lore:** The Marching Woodland, Gorro Tree  
**Leaders:** the Arborist Family, led by Torbadogsan Sturdy of Foot with family leaders voted in when the previous leader dies or is deposed    
**Amenities:** hardwood, carpentry tools, peat stacks, stone quarry and fresh water pools
